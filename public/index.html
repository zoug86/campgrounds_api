NORTH AMERICA CAMPGROUNDS API

It is all here and at your fingertips: All 13,000 US and Canada (vehicle-accessible) public campgrounds, with accurate lat/lon for your GPS or smart phone, campground amenities and phone numbers. Includes all National Park, National Forest, State Park and Provincial campgrounds, all BLM, TVA and Army engineers and military-only campgrounds. Most regional, county, city and utility-owned are also included.

About

The data is provided by:
http://www.uscampgrounds.info/takeit.html

Description

The data include around 13,311 US and Canada campgrounds and they are distributed as follows:

  • US West Campgrounds: 3926 camps
  • US South West Campgrounds: 1472 camps
  • US South Campgrounds: 2125 camps
  • US North East Campgrounds: 718 camps
  • US Mid West Campgrounds: 3167 camps
  • Canada Campgrounds: 1603 camps

    HTTP Requests

    **GET HTTP Requests

    Data can be requested using this URL:
    https://api-campgrounds.herokuapp.com/

    1- Get all Campgrounds:
    https://api-campgrounds.herokuapp.com/db

    2- Get Campgrounds by region:
    Example: US Western Campgrounds
    https://api-campgrounds.herokuapp.com/westcamp

    3- Filter Campgrounds by name:
    https://api-campgrounds.herokuapp.com/westcamp?campground_name=Allison Point

    4- Pagination and Limit:
    https://api-campgrounds.herokuapp.com/westcamp?_page=1&_limit=5

    5- Sorting:
    https://api-campgrounds.herokuapp.com/westcamp?_sort=campground_name&_order=asc

    6- Full Text Search:
    https://api-campgrounds.herokuapp.com/westcamp?q=Allison Point
